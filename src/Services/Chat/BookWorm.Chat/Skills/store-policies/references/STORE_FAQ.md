# Store FAQ

## Shipping

**Q: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?**
A: Yes, within 1 hour of placing the order. Contact support immediately with your order number.

**Q: Do you ship to PO boxes?**
A: Standard shipping ships to PO boxes; Express and Overnight require a street address.

**Q: My package shows delivered but I didn't receive it.**
A: Wait 24 hours in case of early scan. If still missing, file a missing package report with support within 7 days.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes, to over 50 countries. Import duties and taxes are the customer's responsibility.

## Returns & Refunds

**Q: Can I return a book I've started reading?**
A: Physical books in resalable condition (no markings, intact spine) qualify for return within 30 days.

**Q: What if I received the wrong book?**
A: Contact support within 7 days with your order number and a photo. We'll send the correct item and cover return
shipping.

**Q: How do exchanges work?**
A: Initiate a return and place a new order. We do not do direct exchanges.

**Q: When will I receive my refund?**
A: Refunds process within 5–7 business days once we receive the returned item.

## Account & Orders

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link is sent to your registered email within 5 minutes.

**Q: Can I merge two accounts?**
A: Account merges are handled by our support team. Submit a request with both email addresses.

**Q: How do I cancel a subscription (BookPoints Premium)?**
A: Go to Account Settings > Subscriptions > Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the billing cycle.

**Q: Where can I find my invoices?**
A: Account Settings > My Orders > select the order > Download Invoice.

## Gift Cards & Loyalty

**Q: Can gift cards be used with discount codes?**
A: Yes, gift cards and discount codes can be combined at checkout.

**Q: Do BookPoints expire?**
A: Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.

**Q: Can I transfer BookPoints to another account?**
A: Points are non-transferable but can be gifted as a gift card.

## Privacy & Data

**Q: How do you use my personal data?**
A: See our Privacy Policy at bookworm.com/privacy. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Submit a deletion request via Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Processing takes up to 30 days.
